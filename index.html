SOUND PRONOUNCIATION OF ENGLISH ALPHABET

Teach Your Child to Read and Pronounciate English Alphabet

There are 26 letters in the English alphabet, each of which has a name and at least one sound. It is the sounds of these letters and their names that we blend together to form words.

NOTE:- At this point, it is much more important for your child to know the sounds of the letters than their names. Knowledge of the letter names will be very useful for spelling.
